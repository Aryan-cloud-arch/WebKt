Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

Use the logo on clean, uncluttered backgrounds. Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "K".

✓ Do

Use the white logo on dark backgrounds and black logo on light backgrounds for maximum contrast.

✗ Don't

Never stretch, rotate, or distort the logo. Always scale proportionally.

✗ Don't

Never place the logo on busy backgrounds or images that reduce legibility.

✓ Do

Use the icon mark alone for small applications like favicons, app icons, and social media avatars.

✗ Don't

Never add effects like shadows, glows, or outlines to the logo unless specified in brand guidelines.

Minimum Sizes

Favicon — 16px
App Icon — 32px
Standard — 64px
